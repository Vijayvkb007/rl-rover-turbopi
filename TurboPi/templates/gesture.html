Gesture Recognition

About Gesture Recognition

The gesture recognition mode allows you to control the rover using hand gestures captured by the camera. The rover will recognize specific hand movements and perform corresponding actions.

This feature uses computer vision algorithms to detect and interpret your hand positions and movements in real-time.

How to Use

  1. Position yourself in front of the camera, ensuring good lighting conditions.
  2. Keep your hand within the camera's field of view at all times.
  3. Make clear, deliberate gestures as shown in the guide below.
  4. Maintain each gesture for at least 1-2 seconds for reliable detection.
  5. Avoid rapid movements or switching between gestures too quickly.

Tips for Best Performance

• Ensure adequate lighting for better hand detection.

• Position yourself against a plain background for optimal recognition.

• Keep a distance of approximately 1-2 feet from the camera.

• If the rover is not responding to gestures, try adjusting your hand position or lighting conditions.
