Face Tracking

About Face tracking

The Face tracking mode allows you to control the rover using your Face captured by the camera. The rover will recognize face and follows you as a pet.

This feature uses computer vision algorithms to detect and interpret your face positions and movements in real-time.

How to Use

  1. Position yourself in front of the camera, ensuring good lighting conditions.
  2. Keep your face within the camera's field of view at all times.
  3. Maintain your face for at least 1-2 seconds for reliable detection.

Tips for Best Performance

• Ensure adequate lighting for better hand detection.

• Position yourself against a plain background for optimal recognition.

• Keep a distance of approximately 1-2 feet from the camera.

• If the rover is not responding to gestures, try adjusting your hand position or lighting conditions.
